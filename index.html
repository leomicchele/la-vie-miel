About Us
Introducing La Vie en Miel

We are a group of entrepreneurs who have joined forces, work, knowledge, and experiences to immerse ourselves in the fascinating world of apiculture. Since 2019, we have been engaged in beekeeping, and as of 2023, we have embarked on the exciting path of export, marketing both our own honey and that of associated producers.

With over 20 years of experience in marketing and exporting food to more than 25 countries across all continents, we stand out for consistently pursuing excellence in all the products in our portfolio. We guarantee quality and traceability to meet the demands of the world's most discerning markets.
